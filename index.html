Mapping the Universe with Data

About

We develop statistical and computational tools to extract cosmological information from observational data, with emphasis on the Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB), large-scale structure, and inference under uncertainty. Our work integrates theory, simulations, and data analysis to understand the fundamental properties of the Universe.
